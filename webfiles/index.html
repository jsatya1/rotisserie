About Us Chef Steven Pernetti began his culinary career flipping pizzas at the age of 14, and by 19, worked for the InterContinental Hotel in NYC and San Diego. Working his way through restaurants on both coasts, Chef Pernetti became the Executive Chef at the Golden Door Resort in Southern California. He continued on to become the Executive Chef at Cal-a-Vie Health Spa Luxury Resort as personal chef to celebrity clientele.

Chef Pernetti left Cal-a-Vie to join Rotisserie Affair Catering in Southern California as the Executive Chef and then relocated to the East Coast to establish Rotisserie Cuisine & Catering. In partnership with Kimmy Addiego, a Certified Nutritionist, Event Planner, and Decorator with over 18 years of experience, they provide unique catering experiences of all kinds. The star of each specially coordinated event is a menu that your palate will never forget.
