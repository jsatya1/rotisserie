About Us World renowned celebrity chef Steven Pernetti grew up in a food-focused Italian family and started his culinary career flipping pizzas at the age of 14 in New Jersey. He worked his way through InterContinental Hotel restaurants on both coasts. The jump to spa food occurred more than 20 years ago as the Executive Chef at the Golden Door Destination Spa in southern California. He loved the feedback from guests who raved about eating well and dropping pounds. Chef Steven Pernetti became the Executive Chef at Cal-a- Vie Health Spa Luxury Resort in San Diego in 1994 and had been there for 15 years. He has been the personal chef for many celebrities over the years. He has now expanded to the East Coast as the Chef and Owner of Rotisserie Cuisine & Catering which includes all types of cuisines, parties, and events. Co- owner Kimmy Addiego is a certified nutritionist, former National Figure Competitor, and was the owner of her personal training business, Body Creations by Kimmy Addiego, for over 10 years. She has been an event planner for the past 15 years and has partnered with Chef Steven Pernetti. Together they are providing the best catering experience in all of the tri-state area and Pennsylvania.
